Video Game Tunes + Klezmer

Glitch Kibbitz reimagines your favorite video game soundtracks through the soul-stirring lens of Jewish folk music

Listen Now Join the List

Since 2019, we've been asking the important questions: What if Mario grabbed a clarinet instead of a mushroom? What if Master Chief celebrated with a hora? We've taken our musical mishigas from PAX West to MAGFest, put out half a dozen releases, and even created our own games!
